Administration of Shivaji Maharaj

1. Efficient Revenue System

Shivaji Maharaj implemented a sound revenue system by abolishing feudal intermediaries and collecting taxes directly from peasants. This ensured fairness and increased revenue for the state.

2. Ashtapradhan Council

The administration was streamlined through the Ashtapradhan council, an eight-minister cabinet with specific responsibilities. This ensured efficient governance and decision-making.

3. Promoting Agriculture

He encouraged agriculture by providing loans to farmers and promoting irrigation projects. These measures improved productivity and welfare of the agrarian community.

4. Fort Administration

Shivaji Maharaj ensured each fort was well-maintained with appointed administrators, garrisons, and supplies, forming the backbone of his defensive strategy.

5. Religious Tolerance

Under his rule, all religions were respected. He safeguarded the rights of minorities and promoted harmony, setting an example of inclusiveness.

6. Naval Administration

Shivaji established a powerful navy to protect maritime trade and the coastline, reflecting his visionary approach to security and commerce.

Shivaji Maharaj's administration stands as a testament to his wisdom, innovation, and commitment to the welfare of his people, leaving a legacy of exemplary governance.
